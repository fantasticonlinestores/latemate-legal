## 3. Description of Service

LateMate is a playful mobile application that allows Users to:

- **Track Wait Sessions** -- record how long you wait for a partner, friend, or coworker
- **Convert wait time into playful Units Owed** -- turn minutes waited into fun units (e.g., coffees, hugs) based on Pair-specific rules
- **View statistics** -- see weekly and lifetime summaries of wait times and Units Owed
- **Share summaries** -- share Wait Session summaries with your Pair partner

The App is designed to be light-hearted, humorous, and non-blaming. It is intended for entertainment and friendly accountability, not for legal, financial, or contractual obligations between Users.

LateMate is available on iOS and Android platforms.

# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-02-06

This Privacy Policy describes how **LateMate** (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use the LateMate app (the “App”).

## Information we collect
- Account info you provide (e.g., email/name) if you create an account
- Usage data and diagnostics to improve performance
- Purchase/subscription status (handled by Apple; we may receive receipt/entitlement status)

## How we use information
- Provide and maintain the App
- Customer support
- Analytics and product improvements
- Security and fraud prevention

## Sharing
We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- Service providers (e.g., analytics/crash reporting) strictly to operate the App
- Authorities if required by law

## Data retention
We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law.

## Your choices
- You can request access/deletion by contacting us
- You can mermissions in iOS settings
